People Of Uttarakhand


A PLACE OF PIETY

Devbhoomi Uttarakhand, formed in the year of 2000, is a fascinating place to explore the riches of Indian culture,history, and natural beauty.

The people of Uttarakhand are known as "Pahadis".It is a multi-ethnic populated state and people are divided into regions, recognized as Kumaoni and Garhwali.

Uttarakhand is also home to other ethnic groups like Rung community, Jaunsaries, Tharus, Bokshas and Rajis.
